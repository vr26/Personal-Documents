# Personal-Documents


ABOUT STEP AND SIT:
Step & Sit is a smart salon booking platform designed to eliminate waiting time, provide price transparency, and enhance customer convenience while helping salons manage appointments, boost revenue, and analyze performance.

WHAT WE DO:
We connect customers with premium and multi-branch salons, enabling hassle-free bookings, personalized offers, and loyalty rewards—all through a single app.
We do digital marketing for our app it will support salons as well. 

GOAL: 
Goal is to make salon visits seamless and stress-free for both customers and salon owners.

CUSTOMER FEATURES
Search Nearby Salons – Discover salons based on location and services.
Avoid Waiting – Book appointments in advance and visit at your scheduled time.
Exclusive Discounts – Get special offers from Step & Sit partner salons.
Loyalty Points – Earn points and redeem them for future services.
Price Transparency – Know service costs upfront and avoid last-minute surprises.
Booking Confirmation & Invoice – Receive instant confirmation and digital invoices.
WhatsApp Reminders – Get timely reminders to avoid missing your slot.


SALON FEATURES
Appointment Management – Approve and manage bookings (Step & Sit + Salon walk-ins).
Personalized Discounts – Offer time-based, service-based, combo, or customer-specific deals.
Multi-Salon Dashboard – Owners can manage multiple branches in one place.
Performance Analytics – View peak hours, top services, and best-performing staff.
Invoice Generation – Integrated with WhatsApp for easy sharing.
Portfolio Management – Update services, stylist profiles, and salon details.
Reports & Insights – Generate detailed reports for business decisions.
